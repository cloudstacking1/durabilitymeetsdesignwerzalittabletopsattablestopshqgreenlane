Installation & Care Summary

  • Before installing, allow tops to acclimatise to room humidity

  • Use rubber pads when placing on metal frames to prevent moisture transfer

  • Clean all surfaces with soft cloth + mild detergent

  • For scratches or dullness:

  • Melamine/laminate → use gentle scratch remover

  • Solid wood → sand lightly and re‑oil or refinish

  • Werzalit → clean with soap & water, avoid abrasive cleaners
